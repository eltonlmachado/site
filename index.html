FAQ

What topics do your bass lessons cover?

My bass lessons cover a diverse range of topics and depends on the various skill levels. Fundamental subjects covered include Harmony & Ear Training, Fretboard knowledge & Reading, Scales & Arpeggios, Technique, Improvisation & Arrangement, Repertoire & Transcriptions. The lessons are customized to meet each student's unique proficiency and musical goals.

How long is each bass lesson, and how do I decide what's right for me?

You have the flexibility to choose from 30, 45, or 60-minute lessons based on your availability and commitment level. The longer sessions allows for a more in-depth instruction, practice, and personalized guidance. Additionally, I encourage and recommend our weekly lesson packages. Choosing a package not only ensures consistency but also allows students to secure their weekly spots, make a commitment to their musical journey, and enjoy significant savings.

What sets your bass lessons apart from others?

What distinguishes my lessons is a unique blend of pedagogical experience, academic education, and versatile professional performance experience. My goal is to create a supportive learning environment that fosters deep musical understanding, technical proficiency, and creativity.

Can I book a trial lesson before committing to a weekly package?

Absolutely! You can book a single trial lesson to experience my teaching style and determine if it aligns with your learning preferences. Afterward, you can choose a suitable weekly lesson pack.

Can I use referral discounts towards a weekly package?

Absolutely! Referral discounts can be applied towards any individual lesson or weekly packages. It's a great way to maximize savings while enjoying a consistent rhythm of personalized lessons. Simply mention your referral when signing up for a weekly package, and you'll receive the deserved discount.

Is there any specific equipment needed for online bass lessons?

For a smooth online learning experience, make sure you have a stable internet connection, a computer or tablet with a camera, and your bass gear. We use Zoom for our sessions, and while a built-in microphone is fine, an upgrade is recommended for improved audio quality.

How do I pay for lessons?

When scheduling your lesson, you'll be prompted to complete the payment process securely through Calendly. You can comfortably use PayPal or major credit cards to ensure a smooth transaction.

Are there any age restrictions for lessons?

In general children can start learning bass guitar around the age of 7 or 8. Whether you're a child, teenager, or adult, my teaching approach is adapted to suit your individual goals and age group.

What is your cancellation policy?

Life can be unpredictable, and schedules may need adjustments. Please inform me at least 24 hours in advance if you need to make any changes. Refunds won't be provided for cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice. Consider rescheduling over cancellation for a consistent learning experience.

How does your money-back guarantee work?

I stand behind the quality of my bass lessons, and the satisfaction and progress of my students are my priority. If you would like to request a refund, simply reach out to me within 24 hours after your lesson and I'll promptly process your refund.
